SEPTEMBER 21, 2018

Our Client

  • Client Name: Birgitte Heggstad

  • Position: Owner of the kennel

“Teigestova” is a newly established dog kennel which primary function is line breeding of dogs. Our client is the owner of the kennel, Birgitte Heggstad. Heggstad is the mother of one of our group members. The client was already looking into getting a website, and we were in contact with her from early on. After discussing the scope of the website with Heggstad, and the timing of when it needed to be done, we came in agreement that this would be a suitable website for our project.

Website's Purpose

The client’s aim with the website is to have a place to gather all the necessary information about her dogs and newly established kennel. She believes this is essential as starting next year, one of her show dogs will produce a litter of purebred dogs, of the breed “Bichon Havanais”, whom will need new homes. Through this website potential future dog owners will be able to read about the client’s dogs and puppies as well as getting the necessary information about the breed and kennel. Birgitte also wants the site to be informative to friends and family as well.

Website's Goals

  • Get potential dog owners interested in Bichon Havanais puppies to contact Birgitte Heggstad with the intent of buying a puppy (primary goal).

  • Convince potential dog owners for whom Bichon Havanais is a suitable breed that these particular puppies are right for them (secondary goal).

  • Build a social and professional network among other Havanais dog breeders in order to create sufficient trust for other Bichon Havanais breeders to be interested in buying a dog in the future.

  • Keep friends and family up to date with the dog breeding related activities of Heggstad.

Intended Audience

The client has identified three target audiences she feels the website should be suited for:

  • Potential buyers
  • Friends and family
  • Dog breeders

Discussing this, we came to the conclusion that the audience which our client had in mind, most likely covers the majority of users whom the site should be targeted towards. The primary audience of the website will be users intending to buy a new puppy. We therefore intend to build the website with this group in focus. Friends, family and the community are audiences which might find some use with the website, but they are not specifically the target audience.

Based on our discussions with the client, we have defined two main buyer personas among the primary target group:

Persona 1: Anne

  • Female
  • 55 years old
  • Family status: Married. Her children are coming of age and have just left their childhood home for college.
  • Location: The Ålesund area.
  • Interests/hobbies: Anne is looking for a new interest to pursue. She has previously not owned a dog, at least not as a grown-up.
  • Lifestyle: Enjoys a bit of recreation outdoors, but is not overly sporty.
  • Communication variables: Anne uses Facebook to keep in touch with friends and family. E-mail is used for more formal queries. Her 60 year old husband Jan, on the other hand, prefers to call to make inquiries. Anne searches for information about what kind of dog that may be suitable for her, and does not have a specific breed of dog in mind. Is especially drawn towards puppy images.

Persona 2: Thomas

  • Male.
  • 32 years old.
  • Family status: Cohabitant. No children.
  • Location: Stavanger.
  • Interests/hobbies: Dog shows and interior design.
  • Lifestyle: A lot of Thomas’ life outside the workplace revolves around training and grooming dogs, and participate in dog shows. His social life is also connected to the dog show community.
  • Communication variables: Thomas participates in various Facebook groups to keep in touch with friends and family, and to keep up with the dog show community. E-mail is used for more formal queries. Thomas is specifically interested in the dog breed Bichon Havenese, and knows a lot about the breed. He searches for information about which dogs are planned to breed, and if the female and male dog to be breed have records for performing well in dog shows.

Requirements

  • Functional requirements:
    • Drop-down navigation box
    • The user should be able to get to any reference page
    • The front page should have a picture slide-show
    • The website should be responsive
    • The contact page should have a map
    • The website should be Google Chrome supported
  • Non-functional requirements:
    • Every page must load in less than 1 second
    • The user should be able to get to any reference page in three clicks or less
    • The navigation should be at the top of the page
    • The background and fonts should suggest “professional”, “welcoming” and “lively”
    • The main images should suggest that it’s a social, lively, cuddly, kid-friendly and curious dog

Content of the Website

  • Home
  • About Us
  • Our Dogs
    • Dogs(parents)
    • Our Puppies
    • Awards and activities
  • Bichon Havanese
  • FAQ
  • Contact
  • Footer

Home

The front page is the landing site and it is important that it creates a good first impression. We want the user to feel welcome and make the business seem legitimate and trustworthy. The design will be modern, professional and clean. It will include pictures and relevant information for the target audience. From the front page the user should be able to navigate easily and find the right information. We will not make the page too fancy because we expect the computer skills of the users will vary a lot.

About Us

In this section we want to include information about Teigestova and the business in general. We also want to showcase that the breeding process meets the standards and ethical guidelines of the kennel community. Buying a dog is a big financial and personal decision and we expect potential buyers will want to know about the business before making a decision. By adding this section and providing the user with crucial information we expect to establish trust and legitimacy. We want to keep this page clean and easy to read and the focus should be on the text although we might include other elements.

Our Dogs

This section will be a landing page for the sub-sections "Dogs (parents)", "Puppies" and "Awards and activities".

Dogs (parents)

This section will include information about the client’s dogs. Not the one for sale, but rather the ones used in the breeding process. The purpose of this section is to give the users insight in the breeding process and show where the puppies come from.

Puppies

The second one will present the puppies for sale. In this subsection the user can get a closer look at the puppies and find relevant information about them. For potential buyers this page must have a strong appeal and hopefully make the user feel attached to the dogs. The focus should be on the puppies and we will not add more elements than necessary. Adding too many pictures can make the page run slowly and finding the right balance between performance and content will be important in this section.

Awards and activities

This section will include information about the award show activities the client’s dogs are taking part in.

Bichon Havanese

This section has two subsections. The first one will consist of general information about the breed and show the user what characteristics to expect from it.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions. Puppies are expensive and a significant financial and personal investment. Buyers typically spend a lot of time doing research. Making information easily available is therefore a priority and this section will highlight some of the most common questions and hopefully make the process easier. It should be clean and consist of mainly text as well as some links to external sites if necessary. We do not want to take the focus away from the text by adding other elements to the page.

Contact

Contact information such as e-mail, phone number and address will be added here. In case the users have questions or want to make an offer they can easily find the needed information in this section. We will also include links to social media sites.

Footer

Every page will have a footer that will include relevant information.

Final Location

No final location has been discussed, but as the client lacks any previous experience with hosting a website, we recommended her to use a web hosting service for this purpose.
